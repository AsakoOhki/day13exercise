Selling Sunset is an American reality television series on Netflix. The series first aired with eight episodes on March 21, 2019. It revolves around the high-end residential properties in Los Angeles marketed by the Oppenheim Group's real estate brokerage firm.The series tracks the group of agents as they navigate their personal and professional lives. The second season, which consisted of 8 episodes, premiered on May 22, 2020. Season 3 premiered on August 7, 2020
